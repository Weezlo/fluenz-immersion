A GREAT HOUSE

It all begins in a beautifully restored Spanish Revival mansion from the 1920s in the Polanco district of Mexico City. Contemporary art and design mixed with classic architecture in the Cit's garden district.

THE IMMERSION

Your day to day immersion with our elite coaches combines group and intense one-on-one sessions, rigorous hour by hour assessments, recovery time, really good yoga, and great food made with local ingredients and health in mind.

MEMORABLE EVENINGS

In the evenings we'll host you in some of the city's best restaurants (and impossible reservations), take you on exclusive art tours and bring some of the city's sharpest minds for private talks.

A TRANSFORMATION

We'll pick you up at the airport and take it from there. Seven days later, you won't believe the transformation.

ASSESSMENT

We begin with an extensive assessment to understand your Spanish, your objectives, and the ideal path for you.

THE METHOD

By zeroing in on the specific grammar and syntax that are blocking your fluency we give you a path to accelerate your verbal expression and retention.

COACHING

We get you to fluency with the kind of high-impact coaching developed for elite athletes, sustained over several days, calibrated to achieve your objectives.

BEGINNERS

If you've never studied Spanish before, our unique map through Spanish for English speakers will get you to fluency in a way that will surprise you.
